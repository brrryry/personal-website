## Stringing
That being said, when I'm not alone, I'm usually being trained to string rackets. Even when I AM alone, I'm usually
given a few training rackets to string. I learned that stringing is fairly meticulous process, and I have a new 
appreciation for the people who do it. \
\
When I first started, I found out that there are quite a few ways to string a racket. So far, I have only 
learned the "Li-Ning 2 Knot" method. For more information on how to actually string a racket like this, feel free to consult
the source under the image.

This method of stringing is one of the easier methods, but it still took me some time to get the hang of it. \
\
The place that I work at has a nice stringing machine, so I'm lucky. However, even with the machine, it still
took me a while to get the hang of it. Learning how to properly tension the strings and how to tie the knots was challenging at first.
In fact, my first full racket took me almost 2 hours to string!
